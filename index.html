Adamson Medical Clinic is an Irish owned business which has recently opened its doors and can be found at Manor Grove, Kildare Town, Co Kildare. We are committed to supplying the highest quality service to all our patients in a friendly, safe, and caring environment.

We will try to provide an effective, efficient service on time with equal access for all our patients.

We aim to supply individual patient centred care to each patient and their families and promote patient input in their care. We encourage good interpersonal relationships.

Services

    The following services are available:

  • Diagnose and treat injuries and illnesses.
  • Physicals, screenings, and wellness visits.
  • Management of chronic medical conditions.
  • Medication management and education.
  • Immunisations.
  • Prescriptions and dispensing.
  • Lab tests.
  • Data reporting.

IMPORTANT NOTICE – COVID-19

In light of the increases risk of COVID-19 community transmission, we have made a number of important changes to how the practice will be run for the foreseeable future

Booking Appointments:

In an effort to move away from face-to-face consultations, we ask that all patients phone in advance if you need to book an urgent appointment. If there is an urgency, we ask to you call ahead for an appointment and when you arrive for your appointment that you stay in your car and call the surgery on 045 531636. Please do not come into the surgery until directed to by reception staff or a doctor.

Attending Appointments:

If you have an appointment and are over 18 please attend the surgery alone. If you are under 18 only one other person may go with you to the surgery. When you arrive please sit in your car and call the reception on 045 531636 - Please do not come into the surgery until directed to by reception staff or a doctor.

Prescription Requests:

Please allow 48 hours for your prescription to be filled – we can then arrange for your prescription to be sent to your pharmacy. You can request your prescription online using the link.

Certs:

We can arrange to post work certs and social welfare certs to your address. Please give 48 hours for these to be completed


In the case of an Emergency please call 999


Thank you for your co-operation and understanding. These changes will help reduce the risk of spreading Coronavirus and ensure that we can remain open.
